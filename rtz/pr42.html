RETIREMENT OF DIRECTOR

Mr W L Dix retires today from the Boards of Directors of The RTZ Corporation PLC and
CRA Limited.

Mr Dix, who is 70, joined the Board of CRA in 1990 and was appointed Chairman of CRA's Audit Committee in 1994. He was appointed a director of The RTZ Corporation PLC on implementation of the RTZ-CRA Dual-Listed Companies Merger in December 1995.
