Networks

 

Professional networks are a valuable addition to the portfolio of programmes offered to Senior Members. Networks comprise Senior Members in particular professional areas and provide opportunities for meeting, sharing experience, seminars, contact lists, mentoring and other arrangements. This may well become the most important programme supported by the Office for members of the ASM.

 

1996 saw the inauguration of the St Hilda's Law Network which is formally constituted, run by an elected management committee, offering a range of activities and a directory of members. Networks for the Senior Members in government and business, medicine and the media were inaugurated during 1998. An educators' network had its first conference March 1999.

We also have a Career Information Service with Senior Members of all ages willing to share personal career experience and information with other Senior Members and with the JCR and MCR. The Office administers the service.

If you wish to join one of the Networks, please contact the Development Office. (ASM events of course provide further opportunities for general networking.)
