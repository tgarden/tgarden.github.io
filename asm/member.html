ASM Membership

 

All graduates of St Hilda's College, Oxford are automatically members of the ASM. (Subscriptions to the ASM are paid during undergraduate years. A fee of £24 is included in battels.)

The Association's affairs are managed by a committee (Chairman, Secretary, Treasurer, ASM Editor of the Report & Chronicle, and eight members) elected by the members.

Members are kept in touch with St Hilda's and with one another by means of the annual Report and Chronicle. This contains accounts of all aspects of the College's year; reports from College officers and societies, examination results, reports of Senior Members' activities (collectively and individually) and many interesting articles.

Members also receive twice yearly The Oxford Ammonite, which is the Alumnae newsletter produced by the Development & Alumnae Office. Contents include reports, accounts of JCR and MCR activities, news of ASM forthcoming events, profiles of individual Senior Members, articles by Senior Members and news of alumnae abroad.A catalogue of St Hilda's souvenirs with an order form is included in the mailing.

Bed and Breakfast is available to Senior Members during the vacations. Please contact the Assistant Bursar's Office [01865 276888].

The Library may also be used by Senior Members during vacations. Please contact the Librarian, Maria Croghan if you wish to do so [01865 276848].

The Association and College are always very pleased to hear news of Senior Members. (A form for giving information is included in the Report & Chronicle; this should be returned to the Development & Alumnae Office by 31 August to be included in the following year's Report & Chronicle.)

These publications can only be sent to alumnae for whom the College has an address. (Please let the Development Office know of permanent changes of address.)
