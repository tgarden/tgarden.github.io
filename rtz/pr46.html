Retirement and Appointment of RTZ and CRA Directors

Lord Alexander and Sir Denys Henderson will retire on 31 December 1996 as non-executive directors of The RTZ Corporation PLC (RTZ) and CRA Limited (CRA).

Mr Gary Pemberton and the Honourable Raymond G H Seitz have today been appointed non-executive directors of RTZ and CRA.



Note to Editors

In December 1995 the dual listed companies merger of RTZ and CRA was completed, as a result of which both companies have common boards of directors and are managed on a unified basis.

The above changes in board membership follow the retirement of Mr W L Dix, a non-executive director, in September 1996. As previously announced, Sir Derek Birkin, non-executive chairman of RTZ, will also retire on 31 December 1996 from the boards of RTZ and CRA and will be succeeded by Mr Robert Wilson as executive chairman of RTZ.

As a consequence of all these changes the size of the boards will be reduced from 20 at the start of 1996 to 18 at the start of 1997.

Mr Pemberton, an Australian, has been chairman of Qantas Airways Limited since 1993. Mr Pemberton was appointed chief executive of Brambles in 1983 and managing director in 1984. He has recently retired from the Brambles board. He was formerly a member of the boards of Commonwealth Bank of Australia, CSR Limited and John Fairfax Holdings Limited, the Benevolent Society of NSW and the NSW Government's Economic Development Council. He is a member of the Phillips Australia Advisory Board.

Mr Seitz, a US citizen, was US Ambassador to the United Kingdom from 1991 to 1994, the culmination of a distinguished career in the US Foreign Service. He is Vice Chairman of Lehman Brothers International (Europe) and a non executive director of British Airways, Cable & Wireless, GEC and Shandwick. A graduate of Yale University, Mr Seitz is a Trustee of The National Gallery and The Royal Academy.
