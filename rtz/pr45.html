Sale of Hidden Valley Gold Project, PNG

RTZ-CRA announced today that it had entered into an agreement with Australian Gold Fields NL ("AFN") to sell the subsidiary company, Hidden Valley Gold Pty Limited which owns 100% of the Hidden Valley Gold Project in Papua New Guinea.

Under the agreement, AFN will provide Hidden Valley Gold Pty Limited ("Hidden Valley") with sufficient funding to allow it to repay $20,280,000 in total satisfaction of all loans made by CRA to Hidden Valley. Hidden Valley will also pay CRA a royalty on all gold it produces above 200,000 ounces from the tenement. The sale is scheduled to be completed on 28 February 1997.

Mr Jonathan Leslie, RTZ-CRA director, said, "The decision to sell Hidden Valley Gold was made public some months ago and resulted from RTZ-CRA's world-wide review of exploration announced in July 1996."
